GEMSBOK

Gemsbok are the largest species in the genus oryx.They stand about 1.2m(3.9 ft)at the about shoulder.The body lenght can vary from 190-240 cm

The gemsbok is native to the regions of Southern Africa.

As one of African species of the oryx genus,the grassland or savannas and on fat or hilly country are found.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM

You will find the gemsbok at pen F22,right next to the ampitheatre
