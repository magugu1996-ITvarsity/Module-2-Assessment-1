ANIMALS MAP PLACES

KOALA

Koalas are well known for their large round head,big furry ears and big black nose.Their fur is usually grey-brown in colour with white fur on the chest,inner arms,ears and bottom.They have no fur on their nose or the palms of their paws

Koalas live in the eucalyptus forest of southeastern and eastern Australia.When not sleeping,they're usually eating.They rely on the eucalyptus tree for both habitats and food.koalas can eat more than a pound of eucalyptus leaves a day

Koalas do not live in the rainforests or desert areas.They live in the tall eucaypt forests and low eucaypt woodlands of mainland eastern Australi.
