ANIMALS MAP PLACES

LION

Lions have a strong,compact bodies and powerful forelegs,teeth and jaws for pulling down and killing prey.Their coats are Yellow-gold, and adult males have shaggy manes that range in color from blond to reddish-brown to black.The lenght and color of a lion's mane is likely determined by age,genetics and hormones

All Lions are most active at night and live in a variety of habitats but prefer grassland,savanna,dense scrub, andopen woodlands.Histrocally, they range across much of Europe,Asia and Africa but now are found mainly in parts of Africa south of the Sahara.
