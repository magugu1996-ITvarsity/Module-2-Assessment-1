WARTHOG

The common warthog is a wild member of the pig family found in grassland,savanna,andwooland in sub-saharan africa.

Warthog are day animals and spend most of the time looking for food

Warthog are herbivorous and can be found in different habitats including savannahs,forests,deserts and marshes.They prefer to stay near water.
